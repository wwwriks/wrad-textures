**WRAD TEXTURES** is a massive collection of high-quality, photosourced, and seamless textures designed for retro FPS projects, horror games, and boomer shooters. 

**Used in professional titles including:**
* [**Buckshot Roulette**](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2835570/Buckshot_Roulette/) by Mike Klubnika
* [**Eclipsium**](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2419670/Eclipsium/) by housefiregames
* [**Pigface**](https://titolovesyou.itch.io/pigface-combat-demo) by Tito
* [**OUTTA HELL**](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3049830/OUTTA_HELL/) by Lake Vince
* [**I WORK AS AN EXORCIST**](https://pigdeons.itch.io/i-work-as-an-exorcist) by pigdeons

### FEATURING...
* **150+ FREE TEXTURES:** A complete library for world-building.
* **74 PHOTOTEXTURES:** Real-world sourced for that authentic "grimy" 90s look.
* **83 SEAMLESS TEXTURES:** Perfectly tileable for walls, floors, and ceilings.
* **HIGH RESOLUTION:** 1024x1024 or higher for most assets (easily downscaled for PS1-style).
* **CC0 LICENSE:** Completely free for commercial use. No attribution required.
